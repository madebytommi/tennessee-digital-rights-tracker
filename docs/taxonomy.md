# Taxonomy

Each entry should have one primary category.

## `lgbtq-trans-policy`

Government actions affecting legal recognition, healthcare, education, employment, public accommodations, identity documents, speech, or participation in public life.

## `government-surveillance`

License-plate readers, facial recognition, camera systems, device searches, geofence practices, social-media monitoring, public-private surveillance partnerships, and data-sharing systems.

## `health-data-privacy`

Collection, reporting, sale, sharing, subpoena, retention, or secondary use of medical and health-adjacent data.

## `online-identity-age-verification`

Age gates, identity verification, digital-ID mandates, biometric checks, platform obligations, and access restrictions.

## `government-ai-automation`

AI-assisted investigations, eligibility systems, risk scoring, fraud detection, content monitoring, automated decision systems, and algorithmic procurement.
