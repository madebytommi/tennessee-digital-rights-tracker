# Editorial Policy

## Tone

Use plain, calm language.

Do not use:

- “Weaponize”
- “Terrorist organization”
- “They are coming for everyone”
- “This proves”
- “Guaranteed safe”
- “Anonymous”

unless the word appears in a clearly attributed quotation and is necessary to understand the source.

## Motive

Do not state motive as fact without direct evidence.

Prefer:

> This proposal is consistent with...

over:

> The real goal is...

## Predictions

Label predictions explicitly.

Example:

> **Possible future effect:** If the agency interprets this authority broadly, it could...

## Headlines

Headlines should explain the event, not maximize outrage.

Good:

> Tennessee bill would require clinics to report specified gender-related treatment statistics

Poor:

> Tennessee creates secret trans registry

## Corrections

Material corrections must include:

- What was wrong
- What changed
- Date corrected
- Link to issue or pull request
